~ Our Process ~

Solid commits, project milestones and regular updates.

  • Proof of Concept

    Before we get ahead of ourselves we will evaluate what is the core concept of your idea and develop some "quick-and-dirty" functionality to proof the development is feasible.

  • Design & Prototype

    Once we know we are not all chasing a dream we continue with plotting out the entire idea so that we come to a visual prototype that gives us a clear picture of the development effort needed.

  • Build

    The development is split up in small milestones that allows us to get something online fast and expand upon that. This allows us to run a Beta quickly with the core functionality and add less essential functionality after launch.

  • Launch

    We prepare a scalable server infrastructure with DNS loadbalancing and failover so that we are prepared for success. Additionally, we run an appropriate marketing campaign to assure your potential users will know about your existence.

~ Our Services ~

All your needs taken care of

Being an incubator we manage your project under our roof. This means you don't need to worry about setting up shop, lawyers, bookies, government, taxes, etc etc. Just focus on your idea and once your project starts to make money you just reap the benefits! Nothing else, we got you covered!

Seed Funding

We understand that not everyone has the resources to fund a startup idea. That’s why we are well-connected with investment funds and angel investors. However, you usually have to take care of financing a visual prototype and proof of concept, this helps greatly to explain your idea potential. And remember, the more you finance yourself, the less of a percentage you loose to investors.

Responsive Design

The first step in any project is a visual prototype. This helps us to define the scope and plot out all functionality we need to develop. We generally go for responsive design, meaning that the application will consist of one design that adjust itself to the dimensions of the user’s screen. Though its a greater effort, it simplifies app management and assures consistent user experience.

Web App Development

For the majority of the projects we choose to start with the development of a web application. This could start with a Drupal website, or it can be a JavaScript/Node application that runs in any browser, on any platform. The benefit of this is that the user doesn’t need to download anything to use it, and it serves both desktop and mobile users because of the responsive design.

Mobile Development

If your project is one that targets mobile users we have two options. If we decide to start off with a web app, we can later port the app to mobile platforms. Or we can develop native apps for the platforms desired. It very much depends on the specs of your applications and the time-to-market / budget you want to work with. We work with iOS, Android and Windows Mobile mostly.

Desktop Development

Some apps can also have a big market for desktop applications. Since for those projects we usually develop a web app first, porting to desktop is rather trivial. Benefits of a desktop app can be using computer resources and/or working offline. We can port to Windows, Macintosh and Linux.

Launch, Scale & Market

We are aware that a great app with no users will not benefit anybody. We aim for success from the beginning by writing abstract, modular and tested code that is able to scale. Then we launch your app on a scalable infrastructure, and market it to the right audience. We have a great network for marketing via; Email, Social Media, SEO/SEM and Press Releases.
